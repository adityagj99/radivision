About Us

Radivision Antique LLP is an international consortium of Rare Earth and precious metals, unique, antique commodities & articles, space related objects, sculptures, manmade crafts, natural and rare herbs, stones, gems, minerals and mixture of metals, currency notes, coins, stamps, Granthas & other literature etc., and all other related objects bearing numismatic value. We are facilitators/dealers/bidders/auctioneers in this business. Attorney Manish Jadhav is CEO and Designated Partner of this LLP. We have immense knowledge and vast experience in this field.

Radivision also act as facilitators/ dealers/ bidders/ auctioneers in radioactive substances or radioactive materials, hydrocarbons, radioactive material detection & annunciation systems, minerals and other raw materials, and any other energy raw materials, chemicals and products derived from or connected with any of them including processing and re-processing, irradiating, and storing and disposing of radioactive material with national, multinational corporations, companies, HNIs, PSUs in India and situated outside India in the friendly nations with India in an ethical, legal and moral way within the framework and as permitted by Central/ State Governments.
